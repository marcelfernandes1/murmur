Everything, on-device

A dictation app that
actually feels native.

No menus to learn, no dock icon, no monthly bill. Just a key, your voice, and a few quiet touches that make it disappear into your workflow.

Smart cleanup, locally

An optional on-device language model polishes every dictation — removing “um” and “uh”, deleting false starts, turning “twenty twenty-six” into “2026”, and fixing punctuation. The Wispr-style formatting you love, with none of the cloud.

Two speech engines

Pick blazing-fast multilingual Parakeet, or Whisper for maximum accuracy with custom vocabulary and language pinning.

Live notch waveform

A glowing waveform flows from the MacBook notch while you talk, so you always know it’s listening.

Learns your words

Fix a name or bit of jargon once and Murmur remembers it — quietly adding it to your personal vocabulary so it gets the spelling right next time.

Searchable history

Every transcript is saved locally and instantly searchable — click any line to copy it back. Nothing is ever synced anywhere.

Lives in the menu bar

No dock icon, no window in your way. Murmur waits quietly until you hold your key.

Speaks your language

Multilingual out of the box, with custom vocabulary and per-language pinning for names, code, and technical terms.

Works on a plane

No internet, no API keys, no subscription. Once the model is downloaded, everything runs offline, forever.

Three steps, zero learning curve

You already know how
to use it.

STEP 01
fn 🌐

Hold the key

Press and hold the Fn / Globe key — or set your own shortcut. The notch lights up to confirm it’s listening.

STEP 02

Just talk

Speak naturally, at your own pace. A live waveform shows Murmur hearing every word, on-device.

STEP 03

Release — it’s typed

Let go and your polished text appears right at the cursor. No text field focused? It’s copied to your clipboard instead.
